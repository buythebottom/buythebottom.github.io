Buy the Bottom

My Trading Blog on the Base Chain.

The Premise & Strategy:

Here's the theory. People who make good money who trade volatile cryptocurrencies short-term either buy once a token comes out or they buy the bottom of a good token that bounces back up.

Since 99% of coins are scams on base, it's hard to find a good project and be early.

Also, once a token is released, you can't get its information on https://de.fi/scanner/ to see if its legit, only after like 10 minutes.

Probably tokensniffer.com works though.

So if you buy a coin once it comes out, you may realize you can't sell it, because they coded the token in a way where only whitelisted addresses are allowed to sell.

Or you get rugpulled.

Or if the token is legit, the presalers dump on you once the price rises on the first day.

All of this is BS!

That's why we go for a different strategy:

  1. Find a good legit token:
    1. Good score on de.fi/scanner and good rating on dexscreener
    2. Professional website
    3. Unique or good project
  2. Wait for it to cool down and build a bottom
  3. Wait for it to re-test the bottom at least once so we know there's support
  4. Buy! Then sell for profit a day or two later. Edit: maybe it can take weeks :D

This has the following advantages:

  1. The project is usually not a scam, as most shit tokens rugpull on the first day
  2. Many pesalers have already sold because they dumped on everyone
  3. You had enough time to research the project and decide for yourself if its good
  4. If the support holds, you are unlikely to lose a lot of money very quickly
  5. Maybe many other people wait to to buy the bottom and a bottom can be the beginning of an upwards trend
  6. If you buy a token right when its floating around somewhere, hoping it will go up, there is a good chance it will dump within a few hours and you'll have lost all your money if it doesn't go up again :). And even if it does go up again: You don't know if it really will. And it's a really bad feeling to see your money go to 0. So why not minimize the risk and maximize your chances by buying a good legit project when its low and has kind of "proven" itself already.
